The more southern areas are largely arid.
Desert and戈壁 is a term that refers to arid and semi-arid regions characterized by low precipitation, sparse vegetation, and extreme temperatures. These areas are typically found in regions with high evaporation rates and low rainfall, such as parts of Africa, Asia, and North America. The term "desert" is often used to describe areas with less than 250 mm of annual rainfall, while "戈壁" (Gobi) specifically refers to the arid, stony desert regions of northern China and Mongolia. Both terms describe harsh environments that are challenging for human habitation and agriculture, but they have distinct geographical and climatic features.
